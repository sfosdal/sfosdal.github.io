About us

Born out of a love for travel and connection.

The Traveling Goat opened in Lower Queen Anne in 2021, offering eclectic cocktails and delicious tapas-style Asian fusion cuisine in an intimate lounge environment.

We are dedicated to serving Seattle in an inclusive and warm atmosphere where strangers feel like friends and friends feel like family.

Whether you live around the corner or are in the neighborhood for an event, stop by. We’re walking distance from Seattle Center and Climate Pledge Arena, and we’d love to meet you.
