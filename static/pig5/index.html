Pigs can fly, after all

Everett's neighborhood pub, back for good.

The original Flying Pig Food & Beverage Co. poured drinks on Colby Avenue for nearly fifteen years — a downtown favorite folks never forgot. In 2021 the pig took flight again in a turn-of-the-century brick building on Hewitt, with tin ceilings, exposed brick, and a whimsical mural of pigs flying aviators' planes — a nod to Everett's aviation roots.

Today it's on its third life: expanded hours, weekend brunch, and a warm room where neighbors, families, and first-timers all feel like regulars. Same honest idea, still true.

"I don't want to be everything to everyone. I want to be good at what I do — and I don't want it complicated at all." — The Flying Pig, on how they run the room
