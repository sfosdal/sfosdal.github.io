The Story

Everett's oldest running bar.

Bars come and go. The Fireplace has stayed lit for more than 85 years — through booms, busts, and everything Everett has thrown at it. No gimmicks, no rebrand, no craft-cocktail pivot. Just a proper neighborhood dive that knows exactly what it is.

House rules are simple: no politics or religion at the bar, everybody gets a fair pour, and everyone's welcome — and we mean everyone. You'll find the Pride flags right next to the pull tabs.

Come for a beer. Stay for the next 85 years.
